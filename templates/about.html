About the Cat Colony Population Estimator

An interactive tool designed to model and predict the growth of feral cat populations in Hawai ªi.

Overview

This beta release incorporates customizable parameters based on research, including breeding rates, kitten survival, sterilization efforts, and environmental risks, to provide a simulation of real-world scenarios.

Features

  • Interactive visualization of population dynamics
  • Customizable parameters for accurate modeling
  • Integration with CatMap Colony Reporting Tool (Coming Soon)
  • Data export functionality for further analysis

Beta Status

As a beta version, this tool reflects possible outcomes grounded in available data, but it is not an exact science. Our goal is to capture reality within the range of predictions the model provides.

Future Integration

The tool will soon be integrated into our CatMap Colony Reporting Tool. This integration will enable stakeholders to better understand management priorities, focusing on colonies with the highest forecasted growth rates and proximity to protected species.
